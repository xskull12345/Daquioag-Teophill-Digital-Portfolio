Professional Ethics in Technology

Another focus of this course is the significance of behaving ethically in the tech sector. As technology progresses, our ethical frameworks must evolve as well. Key areas that particularly resonate with me include:

Intellectual Property

Balancing innovation protection with open knowledge sharing

Diverse Workplaces

Creating inclusive environments that foster different perspectives

Green Computing

Reducing the environmental impact of digital technologies

I want to use what I learn in ITEC 23 to build a strong moral foundation that guides my actions and ensures my work in technology serves the greater good.

"ITEC 23 bridges the technical and the human. What excites me most is learning how to develop technology that solves real problems while minimizing potential harm. This knowledge will be crucial as I pursue careers where innovation must balance with social responsibility."

The course expands beyond technical material, helping us understand the profound connection between technology and humanity. I'm particularly interested in exploring responsible uses of IT that can address societal challenges while anticipating and mitigating risks. The ethical frameworks I'm developing through this course will shape my approach to technology throughout my career.
