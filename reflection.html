Privacy and Data Ethics

Key Ethical Challenge

Privacy and access to data without permission were some of the main ethical challenges in the course, highlighted under cybercrime like hacking, identity theft, and tapping someone's phone calls.

⚠️
Case in Point: The "ILOVEYOU" virus introduced the difficulties in following laws that do not apply across borders, as well as the legal gaps that existed back then.

This made me think about the possible results of some daily digital activities, and how both unintentional and intentional mistakes can greatly hurt others. E-Commerce Act and RA 9995 (Anti-Photo and Video Voyeurism Act) set rules that highlight the role of consent, integrity, and responsibility when working with any digital information.

Professional Realizations

I realized from what happened that I should always consider privacy when developing new products. I discovered that the code of ethics in IT covers additional duties and requires people to consciously uphold users' rights and respect.

My goal as I advance as an IT professional is to uphold respectable principles by:

  • Safeguarding data
  • Ensuring systems are safe
  • Speaking up for openness and responsibility in the digital area

To ensure everyone's safety and security in emerging technologies, I make efforts to teach myself and help others understand possible risks and legal rules.
